Automating Workflows with Zapier: A Beginner's Guide

January 14, 2025

1. What is Zapier?

Zapier connects apps to create automated workflows called "Zaps." For example, you can automatically save Gmail attachments to Dropbox or create tasks in Trello when new emails arrive.

2. Practical Examples

  • Save form submissions to Google Sheets and send an email confirmation.
  • Post tweets to Slack for team discussions.
  • Trigger SMS notifications when a payment is received.

3. Getting Started

Creating a Zap is easy:

  1. Pick a trigger app and event (e.g., new email in Gmail).
  2. Select an action app and event (e.g., save attachment to Google Drive).
  3. Test the Zap and turn it on.
